Sung Woo Hwang

I am an M.S. candidate at the VCL Lab, Inha University, advised by In Kyu Park and co-advised by Nam Hyuk Ahn. My research focuses on large language models and agentic AI — autonomous agents that plan, use tools, and reason over multi-step tasks. Building on my background in logistics, I am especially interested in applying these systems to decision-making for real-world supply-chain problems.
